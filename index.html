About the Exhibition

Welcome to the degree exhibition 2025, a culmination of years of study, experimentation, and growth. This exhibition showcases a selection of the most significant works, reflecting the students' artistic journey through the years. Each piece explores a unique blend of concepts, techniques, and personal influences, offering a glimpse into their creative process and the themes that have shaped their practice. From thought-provoking designs to immersive visual experiences, this collection aims to engage, inspire, and invite conversation. We are excited to share these works with you and hope you find something that resonates with you.
