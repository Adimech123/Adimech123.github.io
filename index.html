Lighting can make or break a film. There are five types of lighting when it comes to film.

Natural lighting such as the sun. LEDs which are useful for different colours.

Tungsten, the oldest form of lighting. HMAI, that is very similar to tungsten

and flourescent lights, the least popular of them all that has a soft affect.
